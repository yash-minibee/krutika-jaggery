Know Your Jaggery

How to Identify Natural Jaggery?

✔ 100% Chemical-Free
✔ Traditional Process
✔ Farm Direct
  • 🟤
    Colour tells the truth રંગ : ઘાટો બદામી

    Natural jaggery is brown, not bright yellow. Yellow colour means chemicals were added.

  • 👅
    Taste is rich, not sharp સ્વાદ : કુદરતી મીઠાશ

    The flavour is deep and naturally sweet — never chemical or artificially sugary.

  • 🤲
    Soft texture, melts easily નરમ : ચમચીથી લઈ શકાય (ભેળસેળ વગરનો હોવાથી)

    Real jaggery is soft to touch and dissolves smoothly — no hard crystals.
