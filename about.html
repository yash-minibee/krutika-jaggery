How We Make It

Our Production Process

Every batch of Krutika jaggery goes through a meticulous 6-step process to ensure maximum purity and quality.

01

Sugarcane Harvesting

Fresh sugarcane is hand-selected from certified organic farms at peak ripeness.

02
🚿

Washing & Cleaning

Harvested cane is thoroughly washed and cleaned to remove dirt and impurities before processing.

03
⚙️

Juice Extraction

Cane is crushed within hours of harvest to extract the freshest, most nutrient-rich juice.

04
🔬

Filtration

Extracted juice is carefully filtered using natural methods to remove fibre and impurities — no chemicals.

05
🔥

Slow Cooking

Slow-boiled in traditional iron pans to preserve minerals and develop rich, natural flavour.

06
❄️

Cooling Down

The cooked jaggery is poured into moulds and allowed to cool naturally, setting into its final pure form.
